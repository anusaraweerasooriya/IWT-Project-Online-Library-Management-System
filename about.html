......ABOUT US......

The NEW YORK CITY READER'S Public Library is an online library based in the United States of America, with its registered office at the National Library in New York. This online library is dedicated to open access, books, articles, Journals, E-Newspapers and many other library facilities.
The New York City Readers Library is a online library with over 100,000 online members. This online platform is developed in 2015 to provide the Library Facilities in online method. We have been providing our service to the community over 6 years.

Our mission

Our Mission is to allow access to high-quality information resources and services for research, teaching, and learning, as well as to create physical and virtual places for our users.
